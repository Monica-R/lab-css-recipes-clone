Apple Pie

This was my grandmother's apple pice recipe. I have never see another one quite like it. It always be my favourite and has won me several first place prizes in local competitions. I hope it becomes one of your favourites as well!

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup pached brown sugar
  • 8 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in flour to form a paste. Add water, while sugar and brown sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer.
  2. Place the button crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Cover with a lattice work crust. Gently our the sugar and butter liquid over the crust. Pour slowy so that it does not run off.
  3. Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, untill apples are soft.
